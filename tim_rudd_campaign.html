About Tim

A Lifetime of Service to Rutherford County

Tim Rudd was born in St. Petersburg, Florida and has called Tennessee home since the age of three. Raised in Murfreesboro and Smyrna, Tim embodies the values of hard work, faith, and community that define this region.

A graduate of Smyrna High School and Middle Tennessee State University, Tim built a career as a small business owner — running his own marketing and direct mail consulting firm for over 30 years and working as a licensed real estate agent and affiliate broker for over 25 years. He knows firsthand what job-killing regulations and excessive taxes do to Tennessee families.

When his father passed in 1984, Tim supported his mother. Years later, he served as primary caregiver for his elderly mother as she battled dementia until her passing at age 91. That experience shaped his deep commitment to senior healthcare and caregiver support in the legislature.

Tim is a member of Franklin Road Baptist Church, a past chairman of the Rutherford Young Republican Club, and has volunteered with St. Jude's, Habitat for Humanity, the Salvation Army, and more. He entered politics to defend a neighbor's property rights — and has never stopped fighting for the people of District 34.

Where Tim Stands

Issues & Priorities

🛡️

Protecting Life

Tim is 100% pro-life and opposes abortion for any reason. He has consistently voted to protect the unborn and will continue to fight for every Tennessee life.

🔫

Second Amendment

A steadfast defender of the constitutional right to keep and bear arms. Tim will never waver on protecting the Second Amendment rights of Tennesseans.

🏠

Property Rights

Tim entered politics to stop government overreach on property rights. He remains a fierce advocate for homeowners against annexation and unjust government actions.

💼

Small Business & Economy

As a small business owner for over 30 years, Tim fights to cut regulations, lower fees, and keep Tennessee business-friendly so families can thrive.

👴

Senior Care

Having personally served as a caregiver for his mother through her battle with dementia, Tim championed the Caring for Caregivers Act to support Tennessee seniors and their families.

🚔

Public Safety

Tim increased penalties for assaulting first responders and authored legislation to better protect law enforcement, firefighters, and communities across Tennessee.

🎓

Education & Parental Rights

Tim supports parental choice in education, patriotic civic instruction, and teacher rights. He authored the bill requiring schools to observe the Fourth of July with age-appropriate instruction.

🇺🇸

Immigration Enforcement

Tim authored legislation requiring citizenship verification for public benefits and commercial driver licenses, ensuring Tennessee taxpayer resources go to lawful residents.

⚖️

Limited Government

Author of the Regulatory Freedom Act of 2026, Tim is committed to shrinking the bureaucratic state, fighting a state income tax, and returning power to Tennesseans.

Tim's Legislative Record
